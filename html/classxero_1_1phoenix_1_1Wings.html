xero::phoenix::Wings Class Reference

this is the wings subsystem The wings subsystem controls a single solenoid that when activated remove a pin holding the wings into the side of the robot. This deploys the wings out beside the robot, allow other robots to climb on top of the wings and get lifted when our robot is lifted. The actual hardware controlled is a single solenoid that is activated to deploy the wings.
